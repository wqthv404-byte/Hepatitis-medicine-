Common Hepatitis Symptoms

Jaundice

Yellowing of skin and eyes

Fatigue

Feeling tired and weak

Abdominal Pain

Pain in the upper right abdomen

Loss of Appetite

Reduced desire to eat

Nausea & Vomiting

Feeling sick or vomiting
